About this experience

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Dubai's stunning desert landscape with our Evening Desert Safari. This classic desert adventure combines thrilling activities with authentic Arabian culture, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Experience the thrill of dune bashing in a 4x4 vehicle, enjoy a traditional camel ride, capture stunning sunset photographs, and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of our desert camp. The evening concludes with a delicious BBQ dinner and captivating live entertainment performances.
